Classification of Countries

The classification includes 7 levels of geography (North America, ...) and 4 income group levels.

UNFCCC: United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change
[v]: party to the UNFCCC
[x]: not a party to the UNFCCC

Climate Change Data: Explore each Country

Select a country to show various data (climate change, economy size, population, projections, ...).
Select percentile (10th or 90th) to see how the projections in 2045 vary.

Projections:
The range reflects the 10th and 90th percentiles of results from nine general circulation models (GCMs) at a standardized 2-degree grid, employing the A2 storyline and scenario family. Values are aggregated at the country level.

GHG Emissions and Energy Use

Select a dataset to switch between GHG Emissions and Energy Use per capita.
Move the mouse over the countries to display additional information.

China, United States, Russian Federation and India are the largest emitters of GHG.

GHG Emissions:
(in KtCO2e)
= Total CO2
+ Methane (CH4)
+ Nitrous oxide (N2O)
+ Other GHG

Energy use per capita:
(in kilograms of oil equivalent) Energy use per capita refers to use of primary energy before transformation to other end-use fuels.

kOe: Kilograms of Oil equivalent
KtCO2e: Kilotonnes CO2 equivalent
