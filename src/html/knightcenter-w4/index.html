US jobless rate mapped

About the Graph:

I am currently taking a MOOC on infographic and data visualization with the Knight Center. This week’s challenge is to redesign The Guardian’s DataBlog post about US state unemployment.
Here is my submission.
The graph was made with the excellent D3.js javascript framework, for me it was the first time I used it.
Note: I spent some time on the D3 framework to the detriment of the content, as I stated in the section "Remains to be done".

How to use the graph:

  • Click the left bar graph to choose month/year.
  • Mouseover on a state to see its unemployment history graph (since 2009) and compare with the U.S. national rate.
Source: www.theguardian.co.uk: get the data
